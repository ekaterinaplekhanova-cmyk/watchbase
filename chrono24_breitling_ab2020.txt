A Breitling Watch for Every Occasion

Breitling is one of the most popular luxury watch manufacturers in the world among watch lovers. The traditional manufacturer, whose eventful history dates back to 1884, is known above all for robust, functional timepieces with an independent design.

Though it’s hard to imagine today, Breitling was on the brink of collapse during the quartz crisis and had to close its doors briefly in 1978. A year later, businessman Ernst Schneider bought the rights to the name and began to restart production. The company was one of the few Swiss watch manufacturers to retain its independence for a long time. In 2017, however, the British investment firm CVC took an 80% stake in Breitling's shares; a year later, Breitling belonged completely to CVC. When the investor stepped in, Georges Kern took over as CEO. Kern previously headed the watch division of the luxury goods group Richemont. Since 2022, the Swiss asset management company Partners Group has held a majority stake in Breitling.

On Land, At Sea, and In the Air – There’s a Breitling for That

Breitling has been a specialist in tool watches of all kinds since its early days. Chronographs and pilot's watches, in particular, can be found in large numbers in the manufacturer's catalog. However, the collection also includes numerous professional diving watches, some of which are water-resistant up to 3,000 m (300 bar, 9,850 ft). Classic, elegant timepieces with a small seconds, day-date display, or tourbillon are also part of the range.

Breitling offers the majority of its watches in versions designed for men and women. The ladies’ models usually have smaller cases and delicate refinements such as precious gems or mother-of-pearl dials.

Since 2002, Breitling has had all its watches certified by the Swiss Chronometer Testing Institute (COSC). It doesn't matter whether the watches have an in-house caliber, an ETA movement, or a quartz movement.

How much does a new Breitling watch cost?

You can find unworn watches from Breitling on Chrono24 at prices starting from around 1,586.9 USD. These are mostly older models with a quartz movement such as the Colt Chronograph ref. A53035.

Prices for watches from the current product catalog are noticeably higher. For a three-hand watch such as the Avenger Automatic 42, for example, you should budget just under 4,065.58 USD. For the Superocean Heritage Chronograph II B01 44, the price climbs to around 7,158.2 USD. Prices for the iconic Navitimer and Chronomat chronographs range between 8,047.69 USD and 39,910.32 USD, depending on whether you opt for a stainless steel or gold model. However, top models such as the platinum Premier Chronograph Tourbillon B21 Willy Breitling can quickly cost 68,598 USD and up.

How much does a used Breitling watch cost?

You can buy pre-owned Breitling watches on Chrono24 from around 686 USD. These watches are mostly models from older series that are no longer in production, such as the Breitling Callistino, Lady J, or Cadette.

Prices for a used Breitling from current production start at around 3,430 USD. The watches in this price range are mostly from the Avenger or Superocean collections.

Breitling classics such as the Chronomat or the Navitimer cost between 1,715 USD and 57,165 USD. Here, prices depend largely on factors such as the reference, material, and condition.

How much is the most expensive Breitling?

The Breitling for Bentley Flying B ref. J44365 is one of the most expensive watches from the Grenchen-based manufacturer. Only 50 examples of this watch exist. It is made entirely of white gold and set with diamonds (Iced Out). This timepiece is correspondingly rare on the open market. If a specimen turns up, prices of 171,495 USD to 228,660 USD are typical.

One of the most expensive series models in the current Breitling catalog is the Premier B21 Chronograph Tourbillon 42 Willy Breitling. On Chrono24, this platinum watch trades at an average price of 68,598 USD.

Which Breitling watch is the most affordable?

The most affordable current Breitling watches that you can buy on Chrono24 include the Endurance Pro 38, the Avenger Automatic 42 and the Superocean Automatic 42. Plan to pay around 2,858 USD to 3,430 USD for these timepieces in unworn condition.

Are Breitling watches a good investment?

Breitling watches are thought to be relatively stable in terms of investment. Some models, such as limited editions and rare special models, have even increased in value over the long term. However, you should not expect the kind of growth rates you see from industry giants such as Rolex or Patek Philippe.

Breitling Navitimer – Iconic Pilot's Watch

The Breitling Navitimer is without a doubt the best-known men's watch in the manufacturer's product catalog. It premiered in 1952, and has more or less maintained its look ever since. The most striking feature of the Navitimer is its slide rule bezel. This bezel makes it possible to carry out complex calculations such as the rate of descent and climb or fuel consumption – completely analog and without a computer.

Most Navitimer models come with a chronograph function. However, you will also find versions with additional complications. For some years now, Breitling has been offering the Navitimer in versions with three hands, which are also interesting for women thanks to the smaller case sizes.

Breitling Chronomat – A Modern Pilot’s Watch

The Breitling Chronomat collection – just like the Navitimer – can look back on a long history. The first model made its debut in the 1940s. Even then, the watch featured the slide rule bezel that gave it its name, an amalgam of "chronograph" and "mathematics."

It was only in 1984 that Breitling introduced the Chronomat as we know it today. The watch's striking design, with its four bezel rider tabs, characteristic onion crown, and Rouleaux bracelet, is the result of a cooperation with the pilots of the Frecce Tricolori national aerobatic team.

Watches in the Chronomat collection have traditionally been chronographs; however, Breitling expanded the selection some time ago to include three-hand and GMT models. The collection is also home to a line of women's watches, some of which are set with diamonds.

Breitling Avenger – The Robust All-Rounder

Anyone looking for a robust and functional pilot's watch with a modern design is bound to find something exciting in Breitling's Avenger collection. With its bezel rider tabs and knurled crown, the Avenger's kinship with the Chronomat is unmistakable. The Avenger is a tool watch through and through, prioritizing legibility and functionality in the field.

Avenger watches also boast high depth ratings. The standard current model is water-resistant to 300 m (30 bar, 984 ft). The Avenger Seawolf, however, is water-resistant to 3,000 m (300 bar, 9,850 ft) – ten times more than the standard Avenger. The Avenger is thus something of a bridge between Breitling's pilot's and diving watches.

Alongside the chronographs, the Avenger line offers three-hand models and timepieces with a GMT function, as well as a variety of different dial variants.

Breitling Superocean – The Professional Diving Watch

The Superocean is Breitling's professional diving watch. It is a robust and reliable tool watch that looks great both under water and in the evening at the beach bar.

Visually, the current models in the series are reminiscent of the Superocean Slow Motion from the early 1960s, whose design language has been translated into modern times. Watches launched before 2022, on the other hand, are closer to the design of the Avenger collection.

The Superocean is water-resistant to between 300 m (30 bar, 984 ft) and 2,000 m (200 bar, 6,560 ft), depending on the model. The men's watches are therefore ideally suited for professional use under water.

Breitling Superocean Heritage – The Retro Diver

Looking for a diving watch with retro charm? Then look no further than the Breitling Superocean Heritage. Breitling's design was inspired by diving watches from the 1950s.

The Superocean Heritage is available in both three-hand and chronograph editions, and is powered predominantly by automatic in-house calibers. The Superocean Heritage is water-resistant to 200 m (20 bar, 656 ft) – perfectly sufficient for brief excursions into the underwater world.

New 2025 Breitling Models

Breitling presented a whole series of new watches in 2025. One highlight is the Navitimer B19 Perpetual Calendar Chronograph ref. PB1920251C1A1 in stainless steel. The 43-mm men's watch is in regular series production and offers a chronograph function, perpetual calendar, and moon phase display.

The Navitimer B02 Chronograph 41 Cosmonaute Scott Carpenter Centenary, on the other hand, is of interest to collectors and space enthusiasts. Limited to 50 pieces, this Navitimer is a reissue of the watch with which astronaut Scott Carpenter orbited the Earth in the Aurora 7 capsule in 1962. The special feature of this watch is its 24-hour dial, which was also on the original.

Breitling also presented some special models of the Chronomat in honor of the Norwegian soccer star Erling Haaland. There are 500 examples of the stainless steel Chronomat Automatic GMT Erling Haaland, while the Chronomat B01 42 Erling Haaland in rose gold is limited to 250 pieces. Breitling equips both watches with meteorite dials, making each watch unique.

Other new additions to the Breitling range include a revised Superocean Heritage with a 40-mm case and several new variants of the Top Time.

FAQ
What makes a Breitling watch special?

Breitling watches are characterized by their exceptional precision, robustness, and striking design. The brand stands for Swiss watchmaking at the highest level and combines technical innovation with timeless elegance.

Which is the most popular Breitling watch?

Breitling's most popular watches are models from the Navitimer collection. But models from the Chronomat and Superocean Heritage series also have many fans.

Are there Breitling watches for women?

Breitling is one of the few Swiss luxury watch manufacturers to offer ladies' watches in almost every collection. These are slightly smaller than the men's watches and are often decorated with precious stones.

Do Breitling watches make good investments?
Breitling watches can be lucrative investments, but it's important to note that potential value increases depend on a number of factors. The exact model, how old it is, its accessories, and most importantly, if it's in high demand, are decisive for determining whether a timepiece is likely to increase in value. If you want to buy a Breitling watch as an investment, you should read up on the specific characteristics of each model.
Why are Breitling watches so expensive?
Breitling is a renowned luxury watch brand known for their high-quality, precise, and innovative timepieces. Breitling watches are made from stainless steel as well as exclusive materials like gold and titanium. Their creations offer state-of-the-art technologies that guarantee long-term functionality and superior quality. As Breitling watches are often limited in number, they enjoy strong demand and hefty price tags.
What is the most affordable Breitling watch?
The Breitling Endurance Pro is the most affordable watch in Breitling's portfolio. It has an official list price of 3,400 USD. You can buy this watch on Chrono24 for around 2,900 USD in mint condition.
